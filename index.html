FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What type of projects do you take on?
I work on responsive websites, landing pages, and web components. My focus is on pixel-perfect layouts from Figma, clean and maintainable code, and smooth interactions using HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript (ES6+). I also create reusable UI components and optimize pages for performance and accessibility.
What is your hourly rate?
My hourly rate depends on the complexity of the project. For small landing pages or single sections, I usually work on a fixed-price basis. For larger projects or ongoing support, I offer flexible hourly pricing after discussing the project scope.
What time zone do you work in?
I’m based in Kyiv, Ukraine (UTC+2 / UTC+3). I’m flexible with time zones and can easily adapt to your schedule for calls, updates, or feedback sessions.
What is the typical timeline for a project?
A simple landing page usually takes 3–5 days, while a multi-page website can take 1–3 weeks depending on complexity and animations. I always provide clear milestones and progress updates throughout development.
How do you charge for projects?
I offer both fixed-price and hourly-based options. After reviewing the design and requirements, I estimate the timeline and cost. Payments are typically split into stages — for example, 50% upfront and 50% after final approval.
What does your development process look like?
My process includes:
1. Reviewing the Figma design and preparing the structure.
2. Building adaptive, semantic HTML and SCSS structure (BEM methodology).
3. Adding interactivity with JavaScript (sliders, modals, tabs, etc.).
4. Testing on mobile and desktop devices.
5. Delivering final files or deploying to hosting/GitHub.
What metrics do you use to measure success?
Success means a fast, accessible, and visually consistent website. I measure performance with Lighthouse scores, check HTML validation, and ensure smooth interaction and animation flow. Clean code and client satisfaction are always top priorities.
What if I need help after the project is complete?
I provide post-launch support for small adjustments, bug fixes, or new feature additions. I’m also open to long-term cooperation for website updates and technical improvements.
